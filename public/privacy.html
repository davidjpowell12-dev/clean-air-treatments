Privacy Policy

Evolved Lawn and Garden Services LLC

Last updated: April 18, 2026 · Effective: April 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Evolved Lawn and Garden Services LLC ("we," "us," or "our"), operating locally under the Clean Air Lawn Care brand, collects, uses, and protects information when you use our lawn care services, visit our website, or communicate with us. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information responsibly.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Contact information — name, mailing address, email, phone number.
  • Property information — service address, approximate square footage, soil conditions, zones, and relevant site notes you share with us.
  • Billing information — payment method (processed securely by Stripe); we do not store full card numbers or bank account numbers on our systems.
  • Communications — content of text messages, emails, phone calls, and in-person conversations about your service.

Information we generate in the course of service

  • Service records including products applied, dates, rates, and weather conditions (retained for regulatory compliance with the Michigan Department of Agriculture and Rural Development).
  • Invoices, payment history, and account status.
  • Photos or notes taken during service visits.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only for legitimate business purposes:

  • To schedule, deliver, and document lawn care services.
  • To send service notifications such as day-before heads-ups and completion confirmations by SMS or email.
  • To bill and collect payment for services rendered.
  • To respond to your questions and requests.
  • To comply with state and federal pesticide application recordkeeping laws.
  • To share occasional educational content about lawn care, if you have opted in.

3. SMS Messages

If we have your phone number, you may receive transactional SMS messages relating to your service:

  • Day-before service heads-ups ("we'll be at your property tomorrow").
  • Same-day service completion confirmations.
  • Occasional educational content about services performed at your property.
  • Replies to your service-related inquiries.

Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies based on your service schedule — typically 1–3 messages per service visit, averaging 2–4 messages per month during the active lawn care season (April through November).

You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message from us. For help, reply HELP. Opting out of SMS will not affect your service or our ability to contact you by other means (email, phone). See our SMS Terms and Conditions for full details.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with:

  • Service providers who help us operate the business under confidentiality obligations — currently including Stripe (payment processing), Twilio (SMS delivery), SendGrid (email delivery, when enabled), Railway (application hosting), and Google (backup storage and maps).
  • Regulators when required by law — for example, pesticide application records to the Michigan Department of Agriculture and Rural Development.
  • Legal authorities in response to a valid subpoena, court order, or other legal process.

We will never share your information with third parties for their marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as you are an active customer and for a reasonable period afterward to comply with legal obligations. Pesticide application records are retained for a minimum of three years (or seven years for restricted-use products) as required by Michigan regulations. You may request deletion of information not subject to retention requirements at any time.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information: encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), limited access based on role, and secure authentication. While no system is perfectly secure, we take reasonable measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of information not subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Opt out of SMS (reply STOP) or email marketing at any time.
  • Cancel service and close your account.

California, Colorado, Connecticut, Virginia, and other state residents may have additional rights under applicable state privacy laws, including the right to know what categories of personal information are collected, the right to delete, and the right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information below.

8. Children

Our services are intended for adult customers (18+). We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent change. For material changes, we will post a notice or send an email or SMS to active customers.
