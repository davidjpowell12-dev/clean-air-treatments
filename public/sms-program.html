SMS Messaging Program

How customers consent to receive service notifications

Program operator: Evolved Lawn and Garden Services LLC, dba Clean Air Lawn Care · Grand Rapids, MI

Overview

Evolved Lawn and Garden Services LLC operates a single transactional SMS program used to notify lawn care customers about upcoming service visits, completed visits, and related account activity. The program does not send promotional or marketing content. Every customer enrolled in the program has actively consented to receive these messages through one of the opt-in paths described below.

How customers opt in

Path 1 — New customers accepting a digital service proposal

Prospective customers receive a digital service proposal at a unique URL (evolvedlawnandgarden.com/proposal/<token>). Immediately above the “Accept Proposal” button, the page displays the disclosure shown below. Clicking Accept Proposal is the consent action — it confirms agreement to the services and to the SMS program. A customer who does not click that button is never enrolled in SMS messaging.

By accepting this proposal, you consent to receive transactional SMS messages from Evolved Lawn and Garden Services LLC (dba Clean Air Lawn Care) regarding service scheduling, completion, and related notifications. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. Reply STOP to unsubscribe or HELP for help. See our SMS Terms and Privacy Policy.

Path 2 — Existing customers under a renewed service agreement

Customers with an active service agreement that was signed (or renewed) after the SMS program launched have consented to transactional SMS notifications as part of that agreement. These customers received a written notification of the program with the same disclosure language shown above and the opportunity to opt out before the first message was sent.

Any customer enrolled through this path may reply STOP to any message at any time and is immediately removed from the messaging list by our automated webhook.

What messages are sent

Enrolled customers receive only the following message types:

  • Day-before service heads-up — reminder of an upcoming visit, address, and service description
  • Same-day completion confirmation — brief notice that the scheduled service was completed
  • Account notifications — payment receipts, invoice links, scheduling changes
  • Replies — responses to customer-initiated inquiries via SMS

Message frequency is tied to the customer's service schedule and typically averages 4–10 messages per month during the active lawn care season (April–October) and 0–2 per month off-season. No promotional, marketing, or third-party messages are sent through this program.

Opting out

Reply STOP to any message to immediately unsubscribe from all SMS messaging.

Reply HELP to receive contact information and program details.

STOP replies are processed automatically and the sender is removed from the messaging list within seconds. A customer who has opted out may opt back in at any time by contacting us directly — we do not auto-resubscribe.

Message and data rates

Message and data rates may apply depending on the customer's wireless plan. Evolved Lawn and Garden Services LLC does not charge customers for SMS messages; any charges that apply come from the customer's wireless carrier.

Privacy

Phone numbers and message content are used solely to operate the notification program described above. We do not sell, rent, or share customer phone numbers with third parties for marketing purposes. See our full Privacy Policy for details.
