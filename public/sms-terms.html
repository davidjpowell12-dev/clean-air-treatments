How to Opt In

You may opt in to receive SMS messages from us in any of the following ways:

  • By providing your mobile phone number as part of accepting a service proposal, where the acceptance page includes a consent disclosure above the accept button.
  • As an existing customer under an established business relationship, who is notified at service of our SMS communications and given the opportunity to opt out.
  • By replying START to a message from us after a previous opt-out.

How to Opt Out and Get Help

To opt out: Reply STOP to any message from us. You will receive a one-time confirmation and will not receive further SMS messages unless you opt in again.

To get help: Reply HELP to any message, or contact us using the information below. We will respond with instructions and our contact details.

Opting out of SMS messages will not affect your lawn care service or our ability to contact you by other means (email, phone, in person). You may opt back in at any time by replying START or contacting us directly.

What You Can Expect

Messages you may receive as part of this Program include:

  • Service heads-ups: The evening before a scheduled service visit, we will send a message confirming the date and the services to be performed, and any actions needed from you (e.g. unlocking a gate or watering after certain products).
  • Completion confirmations: After each visit, we will send a message summarizing the services performed and any post-service instructions.
  • Service-related inquiries: Replies to questions you have initiated, and occasional operational messages (e.g. weather-related rescheduling).
  • Educational content: Occasional tips or updates about your lawn, seasonal guidance, or relevant service information.

We will not send marketing or promotional messages under this Program without obtaining additional, specific consent from you.
